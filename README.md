# README

Sensical SCCL is a developer and engineering cooperative, offering consultance services in prototyping, open science and environmental participatory processes. We have broad experience working on innovation, research and development projects in the public and private sectors, and in academia.

We provide creative, technical and strategic guidance and support to businesses, non-profits and individuals looking to develop new products and services, or to develop their own capacities in innovation, technology and research.
